# DISPELL
A daily word game.


## Privacy Policy for DISPELL
Effective Date: March 20, 2026

DISPELL ("the App") is a word game developed and published as an independent app. This privacy policy describes how the App handles information.

### Information We Collect
DISPELL does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require an account, does not track your location, and does not access your contacts, camera, or microphone.

### Data Stored on Your Device
The App stores game progress and settings locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

### In-App Purchases
In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. The developer does not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs data collected during the purchase process.

### Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

### Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
